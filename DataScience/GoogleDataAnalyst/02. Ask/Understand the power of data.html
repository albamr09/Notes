Understand the power of data

  • Data-inspired decision making: explores different data sources to find out what they have in common
  • Data-driven decision making:

Note that there is a difference between making a decision with incomplete data and making a decision with a small amount of data. You learned that making a decision with incomplete data is dangerous. But sometimes accurate data from a small test can help you make a good decision.

  • Quantitative data: measures of numerical facts.
  • Qualitative data: subjective measures of qualities and characteristics.

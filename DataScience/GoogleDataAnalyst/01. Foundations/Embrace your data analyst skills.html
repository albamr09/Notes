Embrace your data analyst skills

The five key data analyst skills are

  • Curiosity
  • Understanding context: is the condition in which something exists or happens, could be a structure or environment.
  • Having a technical mindset: is the ability to break things down into smaller steps or pieces and work with them in an orderly and logical way
  • Data design: is how to organize information (typically has to do with databases)
  • Data strategy: is the management of
    • The people: they know how to use the right data to find solutions on the problem you are working on
    • The processes: the path to the solution is clear and accessible
    • The tools: make sure the right technology is being used for the job.
  • Analytical skills: are the qualities and characteristics associated with solving problems using facts

Testing your Data

  • Statistical Power: probability of getting meaningful results from a test (value between 0 and 1, 0.6 means there is 60% chance as statistically significant result)
  • Hypothesis testing: way to see if a survey or experiment has meaningful results
  • Statistically significant: it means the results of the test are real and not an error caused by random chance

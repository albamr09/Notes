Data Integrity and Analytics Objectives

Data Integrity: accuracy, completeness, consistency and trustworthiness of data throughout its life cycle.

Data can be compromised through:

  • Data replication: process of storing data in multiple locations (data can be out of sync)
  • Data transfer: process of copying data from one place to another (data can be incomplete because the copying process was interrupted)
  • Data manipulation: process of changing data
